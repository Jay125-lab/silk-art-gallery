Silk Art work



This gallery is home to the respected homegrown contemporary artists in Kenya and around the world . It is a creative space that never lacks inspiration for both artists and audiences.
Their displays are made up of sophisticated artwork that appeals to discerning art lovers and the gallery will represent a large number of local talents. The artists will be able to portray their artwork without the hustle of moving around the streets or revolving around exhibitions .

We are a young company always looking for new and creative ideas to help you with our products in your everyday work.
